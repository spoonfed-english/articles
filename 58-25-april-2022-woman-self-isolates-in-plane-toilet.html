Woman Self-Isolates in Plane Toilet for 5 Hours

Words: 190 Difficulty: Medium 6/10

She tested positive for COVID on the plane

Marisa Fotieo, a schoolteacher from Michigan, USA, was travelling to Iceland on 20 December 2021, when her throat got sore. She had a rapid COVID test kit on her and immediately took the test. Within two seconds her fears were confirmed. She tested positive!

Her biggest fear was infecting the other 150 passengers, so she decided to self-isolate in one of the plane’s toilets. She described it as “a crazy experience.”

A flight attendant made Marisa’s isolation bearable. Marisa emailed the following message to NBC News: “She made sure I had everything I needed for the next five hours, from food to drinks, and constantly checked on me, assuring me I would be all right.”

Upon arriving in Iceland, Marisa had to quarantine in a hotel where the flight attendant continued caring for her. Marisa said, “She bought me flowers and a little Christmas tree with lights so I could hang it. It was so heartfelt, and she is an angel.”

While Marisa was self-isolating, she made a TikTok video of her ordeal, which has been viewed more than three million times.
