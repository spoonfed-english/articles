Bridesmaid Sues Bride – Part 2

Words: 332 Difficulty: Medium 6/10

Bridesmaid responds to bride's messages

Night Mode
Highlight: IELTS CET 6 OFF
Word Frequency: Low Medium High Extra

The bride had sent a message to the bridesmaid because she had her hair cut after she had agreed to be a bridesmaid. The bride was very insensitive to the reason why the bridesmaid had to have her hair cut.

The bridesmaid response to the bride's message was to immediately send the wedding couple an invoice asking for reimbursement for the costs she had incurred.

The bride still had a dress that was paid for by the bridesmaid, and she wanted it back. When the couple did not reply, the bridesmaid decided to take them to court.

The bridesmaid said, “I was told that I was inconsistent and selfish after spending two weeks helping her plan the wedding shower. I worked with another bridesmaid to surprise her with a bridal shower after our bachelorette trip had to be cancelled. I spent hours helping her out with wedding details. When she asked me to help her tone up before the wedding, I sent her a personalised workout program and even went with her to the gym to show her the ropes.

“When I agreed to be her bridesmaid, I was willing to help her as much as I could even if at times it took up a lot of my time and money. Now I find out I am the “bad person” because I am taking her to court for kicking me out because I cut my hair.”

The bridesmaid had aired her grievances on social media and here are some of the responses she received:

  • What a horrible heartless friend she is.
  • You should have had the lawsuit served to her on her wedding day.
  • It is ridiculous for her to ask you to step down for cutting your hair.
  • How horrible is the bride to expect family and friends to spend so much time and money on her three-day wedding?

What would you have done if you were the bridesmaid? Would you have sued the bride?

Questions

  1. What was the bridesmaid’s response to the bride's message?
    She immediately sent the couple an invoice for the costs she had incurred.
  2. Did the bridesmaid spend a lot of time to help the bride?
    Yes, she did.
  3. When the couple did not respond to her invoice what did the bridesmaid do?
    She decided to sue them.
