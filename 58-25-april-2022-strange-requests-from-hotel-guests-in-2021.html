Strange Requests From Hotel Guests in 2021

These are a few of the requests from hotel guests in various parts of the UK:

  • Snake Pass in Derbyshire, UK: It is a pass. Guests asked what time they can view the snake on Snake Pass!!!
  • Asking if the hotel can arrange a shooting star to appear at a specific time.
  • Wanting the staff at the hotel to get them an acting part in a popular series in the UK.
  • “Can you please get the seagulls to sing and not squawk.”
  • Wanting afternoon tea with pandas present.
  • One guest asked whether a hotel staff member could sing in the room adjacent to his to check if his room is “quiet.”
  • Welsh rarebit (It is a dish that is served on the menu): Hotel staff was asked where the Welsh rarebit lives, thinking that “rarebit” is a rabbit.
  • Another weird request was a room with a south-facing window because the guest needed sunlight to charge his “aura” first thing in the morning.
  • We all know Britons love their pets. One guest asked for a paddling pool for children, so that their pet fish could have a spacious bed for the night!

After travel restrictions were lifted in the UK, hotels bookings rose significantly. Shikila Ahmed, a hotel groups’ spokesperson said, “With more Britons holidaying on British shores than ever before, our hotel teams have also received a high volume of interesting requests and questions, especially around place names, local dishes, customs, and traditions across the British regions.
